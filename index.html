About the Academy

Discipline · Strength · Honour

Ultimate Taekwondo Academy is one of Alwar's most respected martial arts institutions, training students from beginner white belt all the way to international competition level. We blend traditional Korean Taekwondo values with modern sports-science based coaching.

🥋

Authentic Training

Traditional WT-style Taekwondo, Poomsae, Kyorugi sparring and self-defence techniques.

Proven Results
