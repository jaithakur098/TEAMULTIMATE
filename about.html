About Us

Head Coach

Mr. Jai Thakur

Founder · Chief Instructor

3rd Dan Black BeltM.P.Ed.NIS (Bangalore)National Referee

Mr. Jai Thakur is the founder and chief instructor of Ultimate Taekwondo Academy, Alwar. A Master of Physical Education and NIS-certified coach from Bangalore, he holds a 3rd Dan Black Belt and serves as a National Referee with the Taekwondo Association of Alwar.

Over 15+ years he has trained 500+ students and produced 75+ black belts, 120+ state medalists and 40+ national-level players from Alwar.

Female Team Coach

Ms. Kritika Chauhan

Girls' Batch Instructor

Black BeltBPEDNational Referee

Coach Kritika leads the dedicated girls' batch, focusing on self-defence, technique, sparring and confidence building in a safe, supportive environment.

As a National Referee and Black Belt, she brings competitive insight and high standards to every session.
